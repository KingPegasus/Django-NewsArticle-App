# Django-NewsArticle-App
Implementing Chapter 8 to 15 of book 'Django for Beginners' by William S. Vincent 

## Description of Application
This is News Article web app in which you do following things.
- First of all you need to signup if you dont have an account.
- Then Login in to your account.
- You can see the articles, comment on the articles and write new articles.
- You can edit or delete your article.

Moreover, Admin has extended rights, he can delete your article or comment or even delete your account.
